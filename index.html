Our Story 📖

SMV Aroma Café was founded by three childhood friends in Visakhapatnam who shared a dream of creating a cozy and welcoming café experience. During their college days, they spent hours talking about coffee, food, friendship, and life goals in small tea stalls around the city. After years of dedication and planning, SMV Aroma Café became a reality. Today, the café is known for its premium coffee, delicious pizzas, burgers, desserts, and peaceful atmosphere. ☕ “Serve happiness in every cup and every bite.”
